How it works

Three steps, zero friction

1

Sign in with Google

One-time setup. MEmail uses your existing Google account, no new password required.

2

Click or tap

Hit the extension button in Chrome or the share button on Android. That's it.

3

Check your inbox

A clean email with the page title and link arrives in seconds, ready to open on any device.

Features

Simple by design

⚡

One click

No menus, no dialogs. Click the icon and the link is on its way.

🔒

Private by default

Your links are never stored on our servers. We process and send, nothing more.

📱

Android share sheet

Share to MEmail from any Android app just like you'd share to any other contact.

📋

Local history

The mobile app keeps a local history of everything you've sent, on your device only.
